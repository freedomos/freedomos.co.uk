About Freedom OS.

FreedomOS started development in July 2022. Freedom os is still in very early development but the dev iso can be downloaded. FreedomOS doesn't and wont aim to be the best, but it will be another great choice for a modern looking distro for yours or a relatives PC.

More About Us.

FreedomOS is currently based on Arch. As the OS progresses we will be relying less and less on Arch and its packages.
